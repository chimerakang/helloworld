Support for Lumiya
If you have any questions, encounter issues, or would like to request features for Lumiya, please feel free to reach out to us. We are committed to providing you with the best photography lighting experience.

How to get help:

Report a Bug: If you encounter an issue, please send us an email with a description of the problem, your device model, and your iOS version.

Feature Requests: Have an idea to make Lumiya better? We’d love to hear it!

General Inquiries: For any other questions, please contact us.

Contact Information:
Email:  info@haticestudio.com

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours.
