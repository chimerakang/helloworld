Privacy Policy for Lumiya
Last Updated: March 1, 2026

Welcome to Lumiya (the "App"). We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Data Collection

Account Information: When you use "Sign in with Apple," we receive and store necessary information associated with your Apple ID (such as your name and email address) solely for account creation and authentication purposes.

Device & Connectivity Information: To enable the "Wireless Sync" and "Light Control" features, the App requires access to your Local Network to communicate between devices on the same Wi-Fi environment. We do not upload this connection information to cloud servers.

Usage Data: We may collect non-identifiable application usage data (such as crash reports) to improve software stability and user experience.

2. Use of Data
We use the collected information only to:

Provide, maintain, and improve Lumiya's core photography assistance and lighting control features.

Verify your identity.

Troubleshoot technical issues and optimize performance.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

No Sale of Data: We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Strictly Necessary Disclosure: We do not share your data with external companies unless required to comply with legal obligations, law enforcement requests, or to maintain our technical services.

4. Data Security
We implement reasonable technical and administrative measures to safeguard your data against unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Policy Updates
This policy may be updated from time to time. If there are significant changes, we will notify you within the application.
